Apoorva Arbooj

Watching a YouTube video titled ‘Why Machine Learning is the future’ by Sundar Pichai had me pondering with a child-like imagination. He states that better computational power and access to a lot of relevant data are the two main causes of the progress in ML. Working with data - whether it is data mining, data analysis or data visualization and emphasizing it with ML algorithms or data storage has always made me feel exhilarating. I think of data as the blood running through internet cables, fueling the lives of systems that consume it.

I am highly inspired by D J Patil, Former Chief Data Scientist at the White House, first ever chief data scientist appointed by US government. His markers about striving for curiosity, team building, social and local problem solving inspire me to aim high in the field of analytics and data science.
